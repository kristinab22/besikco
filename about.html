about bésik


bésik wants to help people to be more of themselves in whatever they do through the brand, and achieve greatness through simplicity. It doesn’t matter what your hobbies or professions are...just be you. Follow your craft and become a master at it. If your craft is complicated, remember that everything starts simple at first. If you need to go back to the basics at any point, we encourage you!

bésik (basic) originated in 2015 when two working professionals, Kristina Bethea and Krystyna Singleton came together to start a company, originally designed to build custom simple websites to accomdate the needs of others, whether professionally or personally.

While another route was taken for Singleton, Bethea decided to keep the name and idea, and turn it into a clothing brand of her own where people can feel empowered by the brand with the ability to modify the brand to match their every day lifestyle, mood, or personality.

'bé' are the first two letters of Kristina's last name, and 'si' are the first two letters of Krystyna's last name, and both names start with 'k' hence, besik. Since the 'e' in besik needs an 'a' sound, it needs an accent. The logo 'bé' (bae) represents Kristina going solo, so the logo can be changed however and you'd like in color, whereas the 'sik' remains black or white. bésik incorporates the use of word play as people use common terms such as: 'bae' or 'sik.'

For example, "that's sik! right, bae?"

Bethea believes that if any thing gets too complicated or difficult, to defer back to the basics, or the beginning...to achieve greatness. Plus, the term 'bascially' is used almost everyday in our day-to-day speech to explain or make a point in its most simplest form. The site is written mostly in lowercase to represent simplicity. Start small, then manifest.

bésik's colors are red, blue, and yellow; which are also the 3 basic primary colors that make up almost every color we see today, including black and white!

the bésik motto: keep it bésik.
