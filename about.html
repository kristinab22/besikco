About bésik & Co.




"Start with the basics to acheive greatness."


bésik provides quality and comfortable clothing at affordable prices. bésik allows you to modify the brand however you'd like to fit your personality, lifestyle, or mood. keep it simple. keep it bésik.

Kristina Bethea, Founder & CEO


The Back Story

Kristina wanted to use her creative abilities, and start a clothing line. With having a B.A. in Art, with an emphasis in Multimedia Production, she wanted to ultilize her skill sets. In her free time she likes to build websites, play ball, and socialize.

She first started bésik as an idea with a friend, and initially it was started in order to build custom websites for various personal or business needs.

Her mission is to help inspire others to achieve greatness through simplicty by what you do and who you are. Become empowered through the brand my modifying it to fit your lifestyle.

'be' are the first two letters of Kristina's last name, and 'si' are the first two letters of her friend, Krystyna's last name, and both names start with 'k' hence, besik. Since the 'e' in besik needs an 'a' sound, it needs an accent. The logo 'bé' (bae) represents Kristina going solo, which is why only the logo or bé can be changed to whatever color, whereas the 'sik' only remains black or white. bésik also incorporates the use of word play as people use common terms such as: 'bae' or 'sik.'

bésik's theme colors are red, blue, and yellow; which are also the three basic primary colors that make up almost every color we see today, including black and white!

Kristina believes that if any thing gets too complicated or difficult, to defer back to the basics, in order to achieve greatness. Plus, the term 'bascially' is used almost everyday in our day-to-day speech to explain or make a point in its most simplest form.
