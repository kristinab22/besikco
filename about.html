About bésik & Co.




"Start with the basics in order to acheive greatness. Keep it simple. keep it bésik."

Kristina Bethea, Founder & CEO



Kristina always wanted start a clothing line, since she was young. With having a B.A. in Art, she wanted to ultilize her skill sets outside of the tech world.

She first started bésik as an idea with a friend, and initially it was started to build simple custom websites for personal and business needs.

'be' are the first two letters of Kristina's last name, and 'si' are the first two letters of her friend, Krystyna's last name, and both names start with 'k' hence, besik. Since the 'e' in besik needs an 'a' sound, it needs an accent. The logo 'bé' (bae) represents Kristina going solo, which is why only the logo or bé can be changed to whatever color, whereas the 'sik' only remains black or white. bésik also incorporates the use of word play as people use common terms such as: 'bae' or 'sik.'

bésik's theme colors are red, blue, and yellow; which are also the three basic primary colors that make up almost every color we see today, including black and white.

If any thing gets too complicated or difficult, to defer back to the basics, in order to achieve greatness. Term 'bascially' is used almost everyday in our day-to-day speech to explain or make a point in its most simplest form.
