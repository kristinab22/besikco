bésik (basic) originated in 2015 when two working professionals, Kristina Bethea and Krystyna Singleton came together to start a company, originally created to build custom basic websites.


'bé' are the first two letters of Kristina's last name, and 'si' are the first two letters of Krystyna's last name, and both names start with 'k' hence, besik. since the e needs an a sound, it needs an accent. Another route was taken for Singleton, so Kristina decided to keep the name and idea, and turn it into a brand of her own, where people feel empowered and connected to the brand in their everyday lifestyles.


the 'bé' (bae) is a represents Kristina going solo, to build up a company through her various skill sets;bésik allows you to place its logo and brand name wherever and however you'd like, but only the 'bé' can be modified, and the 'sik' can either be black or white.


bésik's colors are: red, blue, and yellow, which are the 3 basic colors that make up almost every color we see today! As everything appears to be much easier when things are in its simplest form, there is no need to make things complicated; but if all fails, go back to the basics, oops bésiks.
