why bésik?


bésik (basic) originated in 2015 when two working professionals, Kristina Bethea and Krystyna Singleton came together to start a company, originally created to build custom basic websites to accomdate the needs of others.


While another route was taken for Singleton, Bethea decided to keep the name and idea, and turn it into a clothing brand of her own where people can feel connected to the brand in their every lifestyle, mood, or personality.


Plus, the term is used almost everyday in our day-to-day speech. Simplicity is golden.


'bé' are the first two letters of Kristina's last name, and 'si' are the first two letters of Krystyna's last name, and both names start with 'k' hence, besik. Since the e needs an a sound, it needs an accent.

The logo 'bé' (bae) represents Kristina going solo, so only the bé logo or lettering can be modified however you'd like in color, whereas the 'sik' can only remain black or white.


Bethea believes that if any thing gets too complicated or difficult, remember to go back to the basics, or the beginning...in order to achieve greatness.


bésik's colors are: red, blue, and yellow, which are the 3 basic primary colors that make up almost every color today, including black and white.

The site is written mostly in lowercase to represent simplicity.

Things are much smoother in its simplest form first. Start small, then manifest.


the bésik motto: keep it bésik.
